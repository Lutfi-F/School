What is Puberty?

Puberty is a phase of development that occurs as your body transitions from childhood to adulthood. It includes many physical, emotional, and psychological changes.

This phase can be confusing, but understanding the changes that occur can help you navigate it with more confidence.

Physical Changes

  • Growth Spurts
  • Development of Secondary Sexual Characteristics
  • Changes in Skin and Hair

Emotional Changes

Puberty not only affects the body but also the mind. Emotions can become more intense and unpredictable. Here are some emotional changes:

  • Mood Swings
  • Increased Interest in Relationships
  • Struggle with Identity

Frequently Asked Questions

When does puberty start?

Puberty usually begins between ages 8 and 14, but everyone experiences it at their own pace.

How long does puberty last?

Puberty typically lasts 2 to 4 years, but changes can continue well into the early 20s.
